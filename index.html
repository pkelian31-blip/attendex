How to use QR codes with attendance
Step 01
Create a session on the Dashboard. Copy the generated link.
Step 02
Come to the QR Tool tab, paste the link in the Session Link panel.
Step 03
Click Generate QR Code and download as PNG or SVG.
Step 04
Project or print the QR code — students scan and fill the form.
Step 05
When time is up, attendance auto-closes and PDF is generated.
Tip
Use SVG format for large prints — stays crisp at any scale.
